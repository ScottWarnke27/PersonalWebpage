Scott Warnke

Bio

Scott Warnke is an active duty Army officer currently pursing a PhD in Applied Science at William and Mary. He has a background in Applied Mathematics and experience teaching Math at the undergraduate level.

Education

Naval Postgraduate School | Monterey, CA

M.S. in Applied Mathematics | July 2014 - June 2016

United States Military Academy | West Point, NY

B.S. in Operations Reserarch | July 2002 - May 2006

Experience

Operations Research Analyst | US Army Recruting Command, Ft Knox KY | August 2019 - May 2022

Assitant Professor | Department of Mathematical Sciences, USMA, West Point NY | July 2016 - June 2019

Armor Officer | Platoon Leader, Executive Officer, Troop Commander | May 2006 - June 2014
